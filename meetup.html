Venue

We hold our meetups on the third Tuesday of every month at:

Axis Cafe (map)
10349 Jasper Avenue NW
Edmonton, AB

Thanks to our generous sponsors, we're often able to offer a tab giving you a chance to try Axis' excellent coffees and selection of beer and wine. If you haven't already, please follow us on Twitter at @yegrb. We post using the #yegrb hashtag. You can also subscribe to our calendar and never have to worry about missing a meetup again!

Getting There

Parking

Reasonably priced parking is available after 18:00 in lots just south and north of Axis Cafe on 104th Street. Free parking is also avaliable along 104th Street though spots are limited.

Transit

For attendees arriving from the University of Alberta, and other location serviced by the LRT, the Bay/Enterprise LRT Station exit is located just outside the doors to the cafe. Make sure to exit the station on the south side of Jasper Avenue. There is also regular bus service running down Jasper Avenue from Mill Woods, West Edmonton Mall, and Castledowns.
