Ruby Resources

This page is a quick list of resources that have been shared at YEGRB meetups. Contact us if there's something you'd like us to add.

Podcasts

  • Ruby5 - Twice weekly. This 5 minute podcast covers the latest Ruby gems and news.
  • The Ruby Show - Weekly. A comprehensive update on the week in Ruby. Between 20-30 minutes.
  • The Changelog - Weekly. The latest news and feature interviews from the open source world. Between 30-50 minutes.

Gems

  • Ruby Toolbox - A comprehensive list of gems. They're divided by tasks and function and include lots of reviews.
  • Rubygems.org - The official ruby gem repository. Get usage stats and information about all of your favourite gems here. You can also subscribe to RSS feeds for each gem so you know whenever they're updated.
